Welcome to the IU Lounge

The IU Lounge is, no doubt, the biggest trendsetter at International University. Stop in to sample the eclectic offering of elixirs, teas, and coffees, or stay a bit longer and enjoy the multicultural culinary menu that combines a harmony of taste, texture, and color with the best in fresh and healthy ingredients.

Our guarantee: at the IU Lounge, we're committed to providing you, our guest, with an exceptional experience every time you visit. Whether you're stopping by to check email over an elixir or here for a study break, you'll find our friendly staff attentive to every detail. If you're not fully satisfied, have a Blueberry Bliss Elixir on us.

Now that you've experienced the lounge virtually, isn't it time to check us out for real? We're located right in the heart of the IU campus, and we've created some detailed directions to get you here in record time. No reservations necessary; come and join us anytime.
